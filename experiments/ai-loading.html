Background

The Strategy

In a $50 per run consumption model, latency during complex AI document ingestion can erode perceived value. I developed a series of Particle Explorations — Neural Pulse, Thought Orbit, and Gravity Well — to transform dead air into a state-of-the-art trust-building transparency moment.

Key Implementation

  • Trust Architecture: Designed spatial particle systems that visualize non-deterministic AI states and shift the user mental model from "loading" to "active data processing."
  • Technical ROI: Leveraged the $25,000 Spline partnership to deliver code-connected components that react to real-time LLM progress props.
  • Systemic Scaling: Codified these interactions into the Evisort AI Design System to enable all product squads to deploy high-fidelity feedback loops via the AI Prototyping TF pipeline.
